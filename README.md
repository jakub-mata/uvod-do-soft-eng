# Happy Plant

![Happy Plant](/assets/logo.png)
HappyPlant helps families and households to take care of their green companions. We remove the stress of remembering when to water their plants and not get lost in the variety of needs plants might have. They will not only be able to keep their greenary alive, but help them grow!

HappyPlant also provides useful insight into the character of soil, rendering the worry about its quality obsolete. Farmers will not have to adjust their actions on a day-to-day basis as our predictive models of soil composition allows them to plan in advance.
